Who I am

I'm a full-stack software engineer based in Cape Girardeau, Missouri, with over ten years of professional experience shipping production web applications across ag-tech, e-commerce, ed-tech, fin-tech, and AI.

I specialize in Ruby on Rails APIs paired with Angular frontends — a combination I've refined across platforms ranging from complex marketplace systems and real-time IoT monitoring dashboards to curriculum delivery tools serving school districts nationwide.

For over nine years I worked at Codefi Works as a Senior Software Engineer, architecting and maintaining scalable client applications. Alongside that, I co-instructed multiple Code Labs bootcamp cohorts, mentoring over 40 aspiring developers across Missouri and Kentucky. I care about clean architecture, practical solutions, and building software that makes complex problems feel simple.
